03 — Collection Process

Methodology

Rigorous data collection and labeling practices ensure high-quality, real-world applicability.

📱 Acquisition Device

All images were captured using mobile phone cameras to ensure the dataset reflects real-world imaging conditions and device characteristics commonly encountered in practical deployments.

🏛️ Controlled Environment

Data collection was conducted within the University of Raparin AI Laboratory under controlled conditions, ensuring consistency while maintaining ecological validity.

✅ Manual Verification

Each image was carefully categorized according to the four classification schemes with manual verification to ensure accurate labeling and data quality standards.

🌍 Diversity Priority

The collection process prioritized diversity in subject demographics, emotional expressions, and pose orientations to create comprehensive facial variations within each category.
